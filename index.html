Help

Writing

  • Click anywhere in the editor and start typing — your work is saved automatically after a short pause.
  • The save indicator (top-right checkmark) confirms everything is stored.

Tabs

  • New tab — click the + button in the sidebar.
  • Switch — click any tab name.
  • Rename — double-click a tab name, type, then press Enter.
  • Close — click the × on a tab (requires confirmation). You need at least two tabs for × to appear.
  • Duplicate / Close — right-click a tab to open the context menu.

Undo

  • Press Ctrl+Z (or Cmd+Z on Mac) or click the undo button in the toolbar.
  • Each tab has its own undo history (up to 50 states). History resets when you reload the page.

Find & Replace

  • First enable it in Settings → Features → Enable Find & Replace.
  • Once enabled, open it with the search button or Ctrl+F.
  • Use Prev / Next (or Enter / Shift+Enter) to navigate matches.
  • Replace replaces the current match; All replaces every occurrence at once.

Markdown Preview

  • Click the eye button in the toolbar to render the current tab as formatted Markdown.
  • Requires an internet connection on first load so the Markdown library can be cached.

Copy & Clear

  • Copy (clipboard button) — copies all text in the active tab to your clipboard.
  • Clear (trash button) — erases all text in the active tab after confirmation.

Settings

  • Click the gear icon to open Settings.
  • Choose between Dark, Ultra Dark, and Light themes.
  • Pick an accent color to personalize the interface.

Export & Import

  • Open Settings and scroll to the Data section at the bottom.
  • Export — downloads a .json backup of all your tabs to your device.
  • Import — loads a backup file and replaces your current notes with its contents (a confirmation is shown first).
  • Check Include app settings to also save and restore your theme, accent color, and feature preferences.

Auto-saves as you type

Everything is saved to your browser's local storage — no account or internet needed.

Open Writelo in multiple tabs — your notes stay in sync automatically across all of them.

Multiple tabs, one place

Create as many named tabs as you need. Each one keeps its own undo history.

About Writelo

Writelo

Simple Text Editor

v1.0.0

A fast, beautiful, fully offline text editor that runs entirely in your browser. No account, no server, no installation — just open the file and start writing.

Auto-saves to localStorage as you type
Multi-tab with left sidebar — scroll when you have many tabs
Undo history per tab (up to 50 states)
Markdown preview, Copy All, Find & Replace
Live sync across multiple browser tabs
Three themes & six accent colors — all data stays on your device
All your notes stay on your device — nothing is ever sent to a server.
